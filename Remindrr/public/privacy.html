Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Remindrr ("we", "our", or "us") operates remindrr.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, create or send an invoice, set up reminders, or contact us for support. This may include:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and business information
  • Client names, email addresses, and billing information you add to Remindrr
  • Invoice details, amounts, tax rates, and due dates
  • Payment preferences (PayPal, Venmo, Zelle information you enter)
  • Reminder settings and scheduling preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send automated payment reminder emails and SMS messages on your behalf
  • Generate and deliver professional invoices
  • Track payment status and manage your account
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • Send service-related notifications (e.g., reminder confirmations)
  • Improve and update our service

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your clients: When you send an invoice or reminder, the recipient receives the email and invoice content you specify, including your business name, logo, and payment information.
  • With third-party service providers: We use Stripe (payment processing), Twilio/SendGrid (email and SMS delivery), and Google Gmail API (email sending) to operate the service. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • As required by law: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

4. Data Retention

We retain your invoice data, client information, and account settings for as long as your account is active. You may delete your data at any time by deleting invoices, clients, or your account. We retain data for a reasonable period after account deletion for backup and legal purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with access controls. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Remindrr uses minimal cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or trackers.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@remindrr.app. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

8. Third-Party Links

Our invoices may include links to PayPal, Venmo, Zelle, or other third-party payment platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Remindrr is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of Remindrr after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
